I am a PhD student in [Fudan university, Shanghai](https://scms.fudan.edu.cn/). And my advisor is [Xiaolei Wu](wuxiaolei.org). My research
interests lie in geometric group theory and its connections to topological dynamical systems and
operator algebras. Within group theory, I focus primarily on the following four families of properties
and the relationships among them:

• Homological properties: finiteness properties and group cohomology;

• Dynamical properties: group actions on the Cantor set and left-orderable groups;

• Geometric properties: CAT(0) cube groups and hyperbolic groups；

• Analytic properties: amenability and bounded cohomology;
